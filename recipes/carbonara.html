Pasta alla Carbonara

Description

Pasta "alla Carbonara" is one of the most famous styles of italian pasta. One may think it is a difficult recipe, but fear not! Once a good selection of ingredients is made, the preparation is fairly easy.

You will only need pasta of a good brand/quality, "guanciale", pecorino romano, eggs, olive oil, salt and pepper.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Guanciale
  • Pecorino romano
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the salted water and cook the pasta
  2. Mix the red part of the raw eggs with pecorino romano
  3. Add olive oil and pepper
